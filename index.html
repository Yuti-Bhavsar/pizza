Est. 2015 · Family Owned · Urban Kitchen

Where every pizza
tells a story.

We didn't set out to build a restaurant empire. We set out to make the kind of pizza that brings people back — not because of a gimmick, but because it tastes like something made with real care, real ingredients, and a recipe that belongs to this family alone.

10
Years of love
Our Journey
2015
One kitchen. One dream.
Shravya and her family opened the doors of Hearth & Heritage Pizzeria in the heart of the neighborhood. No investors, no franchise playbook — just a wood-fired oven, a secret sauce three generations old, and the belief that great pizza should feel like home.
2017
The neighborhood found us.
Word spread fast. Families started reserving tables weeks in advance. Our dine-in crowd grew to 60% of all visits. We knew then that what we had wasn't just a restaurant — it was a gathering place.
2020
Adapting without compromising.
When the world slowed down, we pivoted to phone-based ordering and delivery within our 5-mile community. Our team of 10 — some of whom have been with us since day one — kept every pizza going out the door with the same love it always had.
2025
Going digital — the right way.
We built this platform because our customers told us they missed the window. Missed ordering because the phone was busy, or because they arrived to find us full. This site is our answer — so you never have to miss us again.
What We Stand For
🍅

The Secret Sauce

Three generations. One recipe. Never written down, never shared. You taste it every single time — and there's nothing else like it.

🔥

Wood-Fired, Always

Every pizza goes into our wood-fired oven. That char, that crust, that smokiness — it's not a feature. It's who we are.

🏘️

Community First

We serve a 5-mile radius. On purpose. We know our regulars by name, remember their usual order, and save them a table.

🚫🏭

No Franchises. Ever.

We have been asked. We have said no every time. One kitchen means every pizza gets the same attention. That's the deal.

The team behind every slice.

We are a team of 10 — and most of us have been here since the beginning. Shravya leads the kitchen with the same intensity she brought on opening day, supported by two others who share full access and full accountability.

We answer calls, take reservations, manage the kitchen, and run deliveries. When it's peak hour — 5 PM to 9 PM, 45 orders deep — every one of us is on deck.
